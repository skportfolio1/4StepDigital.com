What is the marketing equation?

Video page

Marketing Equation:

Interrupt, Engage, Educate and Offer

1. Interrupt

We switch people from Alpha Mode (not paying attention) to Beta Mode (paying close attention) by triggering their emotional hot buttons.

2. Engage

Once we get their attention, we "engage" them and get them to read or watch further. At this stage, we further address their emotional hot buttons.

3. Educate

We avoid "platitudes" like "good service, in business since 100AD" like the plague. Instead, we educate the customer with specifics as to why he should do business with you while triggering his emotional hot buttons.

4. Offer

We make a low risk offer for the client to learn more about your business instead of inviting him to an intimidating consultation. Sometimes, we simply provide more information via a video. Oftentimes, we offer them something for free of significant value to subscribe him to an e-mail marketing list.
